1132. A constable is liable to the party in whose favor an execution is issued to him, for the amount thereof, in the following cases: 1. When he suffers sixty days to elapse, without making a true return thereof to the justice, and paying to him or to the party entitled, the money collected thereon by him: 2. When he willfully or carelessly omits to levy upon property; or if the debtor be liable to arrest, to arrest and imprison him, within sixty days; or having arrested the debtor, fails to commit him to the county jail, within the sixty days.
