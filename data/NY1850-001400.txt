There is, in each of the counties of this state, a court, denominated a court of sessions, with the jurisdiction conferred by the next two articles, and no other; but nothing contained in this section affects its jurisdiction in actions or proceedings now pending therein; now does it affect any judgment or order already made or proceeding already taken.

ARTICLE II. THE COURTS OF SESSIONS, OTHER THAN IN THE CITY AND COUNTY OF NEW-YORK.

Their jurisdiction.
Indictments for offenses punishable with death, to be sent to oyer and terminer.
Other indictments may be sent to oyer and terminer.
By whom held.
When and where held, and their duration.
