1826. The deposition oi a witness out of this state, and in any other of the United States, may also be taken before a commissioner, appointed by the governor of this state to take depositions in other states, upon eight days previous notice of the time and place of examination, given to the adverse party, if the distance of the place of examination from the place where the testimony is to be used, do not exceed one hundred miles, and three days in addition for every additional hundred miles.
