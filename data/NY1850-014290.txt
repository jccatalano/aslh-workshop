1310. Whenever application for the writ is made to an officer, not residing within the county where the party is detained, he must require proof by the oath of the applicant, or by other sufficient evidence before granting the writ, that there is no officer in the county authorized to grant the writ, or if there he one that he is absent, or has refused to grant it, or for some cause to be specially set forth, is incapable of acting.
