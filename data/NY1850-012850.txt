1183. In the cases not provided for in sections 1175, 1176, 1177, 1178 and 1179, the perfecting of an appeal, by giving the undertaking mentioned in section 1174, stays proceedings in the court below, upon the judgment appealed from, except, that where it directs the sale of perishable property, the court below may order the property, to be sold, and the proceeds thereof to be deposited or invested, to abide the judgment of the appellate court.
