If, upon the examination, he be found duly qualified, the court must direct an order to be entered, to the effect that the applicant is a citizen of the United States, of the age of twenty-one years, of good moral character, and possesses the requisite qualifications of learning and ability, to practice as an attorney and counsellor in all the courts of this state; and upon the entry of the order, he is entitled to practice as such attorney and counsellor.
