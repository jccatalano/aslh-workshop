903. In an action for the foreclosure or satisfaction of a mortgage of real property, or the satisfaction of a lien or encumbrance upon property, real or personal the court has power, by its judgment, to direct a sale of the property, or any part of it; the application of the proceeds to the payment of the amount due on the mortgage, lien, or encumbrance with costs; and execution, for the balance, against any party liable thereforee, as principal, surety, or otherwise.
