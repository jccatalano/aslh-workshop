502. In actions for the recovery of money or damages, or the possession of personal property, or for the recovery of real property, the
plaintifff shall be entitled to the additional allowance of five per cent. on
the first one thousand dollars,‘and two per cent. on all over that sum,
upon the amount of the verdict, or the value of the property recovered;
such value to be determined at the trial, or at the time the judgment is
applied for, in case no answer shall have been put ‘in. In the actions
specified in this section, the defendant, in case of judgment for the
defendant, shall be entitled to a similar per centage on, the amount
or the value of the property claimed by the plaintifff. But such allowance shall in no case exceed five hundred dollars.
