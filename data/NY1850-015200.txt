1395. The order of confirmation must show 1. The names of all the persons upon whom it appears that the notice was duly served: 2. The amount due, and the amount of costs to which the party foreclosing is entitled: 3. The whole amount for which the property was sold, stating the surplus, if any, or the deficiency, if any, remaining due on the mortgage, and unsatisfied by the sale: 4. If there be payments to become due, unsatisfied by the sale, and also other property mortgaged and not sold, it must be so stated: 5. It must direct that the foreclosure and sale be confirmed, and that the evidence with the order, be filed and recorded.
