VIII. OF ARBITRATIONS.

What may be submitted to arbitration, and by whom.
Submission to be in writing, and to one or more.
May be entered as an order of county court.
Arbitrators to fix time and place of hearing.
All must act, majority may decide, to be sworn.
Award must be in writing.
Court may vacate award in the cases specified.
Court may give costs to extent to fees only.
Decision of court subject to appeal.
Damages upon revocation of submission.
This title not to prevent actions  on other awards.
