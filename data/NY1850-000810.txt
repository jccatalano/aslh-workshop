70. Any such suit, (except those brought to the supreme court, for the review of a judgment of the superior court or court of common pleas of the city of New-York,) which may be upon the calendar of the supreme court, at a general or special term in the city of New York, may, by the order of the court upon whose calendar it is, be transferred to the superior court of the city of New-York, at any day before the first day of January, 1851.
