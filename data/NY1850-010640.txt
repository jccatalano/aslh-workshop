971. The general guardian of an infant, and the committee entitled to the custody and management of the estate of an insane person, or other person adjudged incapable of conducting his own affairs, who is entitled to real estate held in joint tenancy, or in common, or in any other manner, to authorize his being made party to an action for the partition thereof; may consent to a partition without action, and agree upon the share to be set off to such infant, or other person entitled, and may execute a release in his behalf, to the owners of the shares of the parts to which they may be respectively entitled, upon an order of the court.
