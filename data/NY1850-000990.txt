87. This court has jurisdiction also of the following proceedings: 1. To remit fines and forfeited recognizances, in the cases and manner provided in this code; and to continue and discharge the docket of judgments entered upon recognizances: 2. To exercise the powers conferred upon it by statutes, in respect to liens filed in the office of the county clerk, and to control, amend or discharge the same: 3. Of all other special proceedings, in the city and county of New-York, of which the county courts have jurisdiction in their counties: 4. To exercise the powers conferred upon it, by other provisions of this code.
