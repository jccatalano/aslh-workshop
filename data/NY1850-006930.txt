633. Proof of the service of the summons, and of the complaint or notice, if any, accompanying the same must be as follows : 1. If served by the sheriff, his certificate thereof; or 2. If by any other person, his affidavit thereof; or 3. In case of publication, the affidavit of the printer, or his foreman, or principal clerk, showing the same; and an affidavit of a deposit of a copy of the summons in the post-office, if the same shall have been deposited: or 4. The written admission of the defendant: In case of service, otherwise than by publication, the certificate, affidavit or admission must state the time and place of the service.
