536. The following papers must be annexed together and filed by the clerk, within two days after the filing of the case, or within such further time as the court or a judge thereof may prescribe; 1. The accusation: 2. The objections or answer thereto: 3. The case mentioned in the last section: 4. A copy of the entries upon the minutes of the court, relating to the accusation, and the proceedings thereon. including the order of suspension or removal.
