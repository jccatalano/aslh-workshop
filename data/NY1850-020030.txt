1835. If a commission have not been issued, and it appear to a judge of the supreme court, county judge, or justice, by affidavit satisfactory to him: 1. That the testimony of the witness is material to either party: 2. That a commission to take the testimony of such witness has not been issued: 3. That according to the law of the state where the action or special proceeding is pending, the deposition of a witness taken under such circumstances, and before such judge or justice, will be received in the action or proceeding, he must issue his subpoena, requiring the witness to appear and testify before him at a specified time and place.
