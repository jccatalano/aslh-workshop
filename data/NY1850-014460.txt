1327. If it appear, that the party has been legally committed for a criminal offense, or if he appear by the testimony offered with the return, or upon the hearing thereof; to be guilty of such an offense, although the commitment be irregular, he may be forthwith remanded to the custody, or placed under the restraint, from which he was taken, if the person under whose custody or restraint he was, be legally entitled thereto; if not so entitled, he must be committed, to the custody of the officer or person so entitled.
