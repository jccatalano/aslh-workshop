Upon such summons, the party summoned may answer within the time specified therein, denying the judgment, or setting up any defense which may have arisen subsequently; and in addition thereto, if he be proceeded against according to section 1213, he may make the same defense which might have been originally made to the action, except the statute of limitations; if he be proceeded against according to section l214, he may make the same defense, which he might have made man action upon the judgment.
