III. ISSUES, AND THE MODE OF TRIAL.

Issues defined.
Issues of law.
Issues of fact. Both on same pleadings.
Trial defined.
Issues of law to be tried by court or referred.
Certain issues of fact to be tried by jury, or referred.
Other issues tried by court or referred.
Issues triable by court to be before one judge.
Either party may give notice of trial, and place case on calendar.
Order in which issues must be tried.
Either party may bring on trial.
When several defendants, separate trials allowed.
Affidavit to put over trial.
Plaintiff to furnish copies of summons and pleadings.
