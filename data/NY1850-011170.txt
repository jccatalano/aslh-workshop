VII. ACTIONS ON OFFICIAL SECURITIES, AND FOR FINES AND FORFEITURES.

Official bond to the people, construed severally.
Any person aggrieved may prosecute.
Leave of court must be obtained.
A judgment in favor of one does not preclude another action for another cause.
Aggregate of recoveries not to exceed the undertaking.
Execution to be first enforced against principal.
Amount collected to be limited by the undertaking.
Fines and forfeitures how prosecuted.
Amount to be recovered in certain cases.
Effect of recovery by collusion.
When not specially granted to belong to treasury, and property forfeited recovered by action.
