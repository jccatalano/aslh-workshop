651. When the answer contains new matter constituting a counterclaim, the plaintifff may within twenty days, reply to such new matter, denying each allegation controverted by him, or any knowledge or information thereof sufficient to form a belief; and he may allege, in ordinary and concise language, without repetition, and in such a manner as to enable a person of common understanding to know what is intended, any new matter, not inconsistent with the complaint, constituting a defense to the counterclaim.
