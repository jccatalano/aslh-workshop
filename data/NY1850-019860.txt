If such affidavit be made in an action or special proceeding pending in a court, it may be filed with the court, or a clerk thereof, if not so made it may be filed with a clerk of the county, where the newspaper is printed. In either case the original affidavit, or a copy thereof, certified by the court or clerk having it in custody, is primary evidence of the facts stated therein.
