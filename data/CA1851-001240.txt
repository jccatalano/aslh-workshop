115. On granting an injunction, the Court or Judge shall require,
except where the people of the State are a party plaintid', a written

undertaking, on the part of the plaintid', with sufiicient sureties, to the
effect that the plaintifff will pay to the party enjoined such damages, not
exceeding an amount to be specified, as such party may sustain by
reason of the injunction, if the Court finally decide that the plaintifff was
not entitled thereto.
