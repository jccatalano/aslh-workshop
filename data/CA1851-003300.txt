302. When security is taken by the referees on a sale, and the
parties interested in such security, by an instrument in writing under
their hands delivered to the referees, agree upon the shares and proportions to which they are respectively entitled; or when shares and proportions have been previously adjudged by the Court, such securities
shall be taken in the names of, and payable to, the parties respectively
entitled thereto ; and shall be delivered to such parties upon their
receipt therefore. Such agreement and receipt shall be returned and filed
with the Clerk.
