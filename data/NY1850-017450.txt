1598. The application is to be heard in the same manner as any other motion, and if it be shown to the satisfaction of the court, that the allegations of the application are true, that the applicant is insolvent, that he does not conceal any of his property and that he has not after knowing his insolvency, and within six months before his application, given a preference to a creditor for an antecedent debt, by any payment or disposition of his property, the court may make an order, declaring that the applicant is an insolvent debtor, and appointing a receiver of his property.
