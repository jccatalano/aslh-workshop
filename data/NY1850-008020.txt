729. The sheriff, to whom the order is directed and delivered, must execute the same without delay, as follows: 1. Real property must be attached, by leaving with the occupant thereof, or if there be no occupant, in a conspicuous place thereon, a copy of the order certified by the sheriff: 2. Personal property, capable of manual delivery to the sheriff must be attached by taking it into his custody: 3. Other personal property must be attached by leaving a certified copy of the order, and a notice specifying the property attached, with a person holding the same, or as to a debt with the debtor, or as to stock or interest in stock of a corporation, with the president or other head of the same, or the secretary, cashier, or managing agent thereof. The sheriff must make a full inventory of the property attached, and return the same with the order of attachment.
