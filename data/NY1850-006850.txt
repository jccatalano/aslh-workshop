625. In the case of a defendant against whom no personal claim is made, the plaintifff may deliver to him with the summons, a notice subscribed by the plaintifff or his attorney, setting forth the general object of the action, a brief description of the property, affected by it, if it effect specific real or personal property, and that no personal claim is made against such defendant. If a defendant, on whom such notice is served, unreasonably defend the action he must pay costs to the plaintifff.
