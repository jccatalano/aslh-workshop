895. Damages for withholding the property recovered, can in no case exceed the fair value of the property, (exclusive of the use of improvements, made by the defendant,) for a period not exceeding six years; and when permanent improvements have been made by a defendant, or those under whom he claims, holding under color of title, adversely to the claims of the plaintifff, in good faith, the value thereof must be allowed as a set-off against the damages of the plaintifff, for the use of the property.
