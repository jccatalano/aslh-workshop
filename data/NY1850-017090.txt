1565. The overseer of the poor of the town, or the superintendent of the poor of the county, where the insane person or habitual drunkard resides, or any member of the family of such insane person or drunkard, or any person related to him by blood or marriage, who, in case of his death, intestate, would be entitled to any portion of his property, may apply to the surrogate for the appointment of the committee.
