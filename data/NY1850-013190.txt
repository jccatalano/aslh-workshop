I. PROCEEDINGS AGAINST JOINT DEBTORS, HEIRS, DEVISEES, LEGATEES AND TENANTS HOLDING UNDER A JUDGMENT DEBTOR.

Parties not summoned in an action on joint obligation, may be summoned after judgment.
If judgment debtor die, his representatives may be summoned.
Contents of summons.
Affidavit of amount due required.
Party summoned may defend by answer.
Reply and trial as in other actions.
Reply and answer to be verified.
