241. After the issuing or return of an execution against property
of the judgment debtor, or of any one of several debtors in the same
judgment, or upon proof by affidavit or otherwise, to the satisfaction of
the Judge, that any person or corporation has property of such judgment debtor, or is indebted to him in an amount exceeding fifty dollars,

the Judge may, by an order, require such person or corporation, or
any odicer or member thereof, to appear at a specified time and place,
before him or a referee appointed by him, and answer concerning the
same. ~
